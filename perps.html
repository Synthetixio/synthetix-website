Synthetix Perps

Synthetix perpetual futures are a set of permissionless and decentralized smart contracts, offering deep liquidity and low trading fees on a range of synthetic assets

All supported markets benefit from deep liquidity and low fees, thanks to the use of fully decentralized off-chain oracles and Synthetix liquidity.
